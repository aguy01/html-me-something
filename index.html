Stamp Collection is a cool Hobby

Stamps say a lot about the people, culture, location-its geography, and historical events.Its a learning tool of the world around me.

Stamps can be classified under three categories.

  • Postage stamps
  • Commemorative stamps
  • Definitive stamps

Postage Stamps

They are self adhesive made out of paper. It displays on mail item with purchased amount printed. They are in different geometrical shapes such as, triangle, square, circle, and rectangle.

Commemorative Stamps

It shows importance of a place, event, person or object. Title of the stamp reminds reader of historical importance. This type of stamps are available for limited time.

Definitive Stamps

Its in everyday use for postage purposes and it remains on sale for extended amount of time. They are in different denominations such as in US, 1cent, 5 cents, nickel, dime, etc.

Stamps can be stored on a paper sheet or in album. They need to be portected from dust and harsh environments to keep them healthy. Over a period of time stamps do become very valubale based on its rarity and conditon.

I like collecting stamps. Its a fun hobbby.
